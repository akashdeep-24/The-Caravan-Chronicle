🚀 Caravan Chronicle

Caravan Chronicle is a smart civic complaint management system designed to connect citizens with municipal authorities. It streamlines the process of reporting, tracking, and resolving public issues while ensuring transparency, accountability, and efficiency in governance.

📌 Problem Statement

Citizens often face delays, lack of transparency, and poor communication when reporting civic issues like road damage, water leakage, or waste management. Caravan Chronicle solves this by providing a centralized digital platform for complaint handling.

🚀 Caravan Chronicle

Caravan Chronicle is a smart civic complaint management platform designed to streamline communication between citizens and municipal authorities. The system enables users to report local issues efficiently while ensuring transparency, accountability, and timely resolution of complaints.

Citizens can register securely, submit complaints related to issues such as road damage, water leakage, or waste management, and track their progress in real time. Each complaint follows a structured lifecycle — OPEN → IN PROGRESS → RESOLVED — allowing both users and authorities to monitor its status clearly.

On the administrative side, the platform provides a powerful municipal dashboard where staff can view, assign, and update complaints. Advanced search and filtering options allow efficient management based on category, location, urgency, or date. Role-based access control ensures that different users (citizens, staff, and administrators) interact with the system according to their responsibilities.

Caravan Chronicle also incorporates advanced features to enhance efficiency and governance. Heatmap visualization provides insights into complaint density across different areas, helping authorities identify problem hotspots. SLA tracking ensures that complaints are resolved within defined timeframes, while an escalation system automatically flags overdue issues. A public transparency portal displays real-time statistics such as resolution rates and pending complaints, promoting accountability.

Additionally, the platform supports automated notifications via SMS, email, or push alerts to keep users informed about status updates and delays. Export and reporting features enable authorities to generate CSV/PDF reports for analysis and decision-making.

Overall, Caravan Chronicle aims to build a transparent, efficient, and citizen-centric system that improves urban service delivery and strengthens trust between the public and governing bodies.
